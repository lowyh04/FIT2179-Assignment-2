3. Malaysia Export Analysis

Malaysia Export Products Growth Rate (2025)

Electrical Machinery and Pharmaceutical Products recorded strong positive growth, indicating robust demand for high-value manufactured exports. Meanwhile, resource-based sectors such as Mineral Fuels and Iron & Steel experienced negative growth, reflecting global commodity fluctuations.

Sectoral Contribution to Malaysia’s Total Exports (2024)

Machinery and Technology contributed the largest export share overall. Energy and Resources remained important to Malaysia’s trade economy. Export values varied significantly across different industrial sectors.
