1. Overview of Malaysia International Trade

Yearly Imports and Exports Value (2019 - 2025)

Malaysia’s trade steadily increased across recent years. Exports and imports grew despite global disruptions. The chart highlights long-term trade performance trends.

Malaysia Trade-to-GDP Ratio, GDP and Exchange Rate Trends (1990–2023)

Malaysia's GDP generally increased over the past three decades. Exchange rates fluctuated in response to economic events and market conditions. Bubble sizes illustrate the importance of trade to the Malaysian economy.

Malaysia Yearly Trade Balance Difference (2015 - 2025)

Trade balance improvements were observed from 2017 to 2021. The strongest increase occurred in 2021, contributing substantially to overall growth. Subsequent declines from 2022 onwards offset many of the earlier gains.

3. Malaysia Export Analysis

Malaysia Export Products Growth Rate (2025)

Electrical Machinery and Pharmaceutical Products recorded strong positive growth, indicating robust demand for high-value manufactured exports. Meanwhile, resource-based sectors such as Mineral Fuels and Iron & Steel experienced negative growth, reflecting global commodity fluctuations.

Sectoral Contribution to Malaysia’s Total Exports (2024)
